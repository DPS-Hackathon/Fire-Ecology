What is Fire Ecology?

Fire ecology is the scientific study of the role of fire in ecosystems and the relationships between fire, the environment, and living organisms. It involves understanding how fire shapes landscapes, influences plant and animal communities, and affects ecological processes. Fire has been a natural component of many ecosystems for millions of years, and fire ecology seeks to uncover the complex interactions between fire and the environment.
